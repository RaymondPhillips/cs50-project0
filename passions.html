Mechanical Keyboards

Keyboards come with different types of switches. Most laptops are made of chicklet type switches and the vast majority of computer keyboards use a rubber dome switch. Rubber domes are not satisfying to type on, so for the people interested in the typing experience, let me introduce you to the mechanical swithces. They come in tactile, linear, or clicky. Mechanical switches feel way better to type on than rubber dome switches.
